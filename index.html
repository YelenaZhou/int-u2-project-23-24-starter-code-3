There is a perception that gasoline powered cars are not that bad.

Affects Who?

“Emissions from cars, trucks and buses make up more than 40 percent of the state’s greenhouse gas emissions.”

Emissions from gasoline powered vehicles are major part of California’s greenhouse gas emissions. Gasoline powered cars are affecting the state. Those emissions lead to climate change which are already impacting people and communities throughout the state: In 2020, Oakland, California had historic wildfires, weeks of suffocating smoke and record-breaking heat waves that had lasting effects on the health of people living in the communities.

Impacts

“One reason for air pollution and climate change is vehicular emission. For example, while in 2000 around 800,000 people died from air pollution, this number rose to 3.2 million people in 2010.”

The perception of gasoline powered cars are not that bad is a big problem because it makes people drive gasoline cars and that causes air pollution, which leads to many people dying. If the perception still exists, then there will be more people died from the emissions created by gasoline powered cars.

“The researchers found that, on average, gasoline cars emit more than 350 grams of CO2 per mile driven over their lifetimes.”

The CO2 emissions from gasoline powered cars are high in their lifetimes. And CO2 are bad for human health because it will replace nitrogen and oxygen when CO2 emissions reach a certain level. And they are also bad for the environment, because these CO2 emissions accelerate global warming. This perception leads to the way some people drive which has lingering impacts on human health and climate change. And it is important to know that climate change leads to many extreme weather events, air pollutions, and also mental health issues.

Solution

“The report says that people in the US would have 2.2 million fewer asthma attacks and 10.7 million fewer lost workdays, and the country would net $978 billion in public health benefits with the move to cleaner vehicles and a cleaner power supply.”

The benefits of changing from gasoline powered cars to cleaner vehicles and power supply will get people to start to change their perception because they know they will have a healthier life and a higher quality life. Reducing the NO2 emissions created by gasoline powered cars can reduce the number of asthma attacks that are caused by air pollution.
